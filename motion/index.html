Wichita Riverflix Nights

Free Outdoor Movie Nights with your loved ones

What is the event about?

Riverflix Nights brings the community together for a series of free, family‑friendly movies projected onto a giant screen at the Wichit Foundation Ampitheater every Friday night. Bring a blanket, snacks, and enjoy a night of cinema under the sky.

When and Where

  • Date: Fridays, April 24 – October 30, 2026
  • Time: 6:00 pm – 10:00 pm
  • Location: 300 N McLean Blvd Wichita, KS 67203
