Local Korean Restaurant: Kimchi

Warm, family-owned Korean restaurant

Kimchi is a warm, family-owned Korean eatery located in Wichita. The restaurant has a cozy and inviting atmosphere, with traditional Korean dishes and friendly staff. The menu features a variety of authentic Korean dishes, including bibimbap, bulgogi, and of course, kimchi. The ingredients are fresh and flavorful. Whether you're a fan of Korean cuisine or looking to try something new, Kimchi is a must-visit restaurant that will satisfy your cravings for delicious and authentic Korean food. Some of my favorite dishes are the tteokbokki and kimchi stew.
