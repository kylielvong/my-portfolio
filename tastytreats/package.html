Description

Snickerdoodle cookies are a classic that everyone loves. This is easy cookie recipe was passed down from my grandmother and is a favorite in our family. What makes this recipe special is the perfect balance of texture and flavor, soft centers, slightly crisp edges, and a delightful cinnamon sugar coating. They are easy to bake and make a perfect treat for any occasion.
